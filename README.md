### 🔑 Key Features
- **Transaction Analysis**: Cleaned and modeled data with relationships across customers, products, and sales.  
- **KPIs**: Total Sales, Quantity, Profit, and Average Order Quantity.  
- **Visualizations**:  
  - Sales by Category, Subcategory, Region, and State.  
  - Profit and Sales trends by Month.  
  - Forecasting model for 15-day sales prediction.  
  - Payment and Shipping mode breakdowns.  
- **Dashboard Design**: Interactive slicers, maps, and charts for dynamic exploration.  

### 🎯 Purpose
The goal is to practice **data cleaning, modeling, DAX measures, and dashboard design** in Power BI, simulating real-world business reporting and forecasting scenarios.
